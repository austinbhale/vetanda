about us

Vetanda Dance Collective is an entertainment company comprised of dancers residing throughout the New York City area. With emphasis on creating an immersive experience, Vetanda's jazz inspired movement puts a contemporary twist on conventional cabaret style performances. Vetanda aims to continue challenging and exploring the role of dance in nightlife settings to further enhance the audience experience. Since its debut, Vetanda has performed at a range of venues around Manhattan, such as The Norwood Club, The Duplex, and Coney Island Baby. They have also been featured in music videos by musical artists Diana Bidèa and Downaman.
